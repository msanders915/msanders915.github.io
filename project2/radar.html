Radar Detectors

One way that you can reduce your chances of being pulled over in the first place is by purchasing a radar detector. It is a device legal in 48 US states (all but Washington DC and Virginia) that when mounted in a vehicle, will signal the driver of radar waves from police cars.All you have to do is mount the device to your windshield and turn it on every time you drive, and when you hear it beep, check that your going the speed limit and watch for a cop.
