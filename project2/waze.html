Waze: A useful tool

Disclaimer: We do not condone speeding or reckless driving. However, if you are going to make bad decisions, be smart about how you do it.

Waze relies on the collabaration of Wazers in your area reporting incidents like a stopped car, traffic, or speed traps. This can be very useful in allowing one to prepare for anything they may encounter on the road. This promotes not only safe driving, but can save one from an expensive ticket.
