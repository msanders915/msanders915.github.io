About


Vickie Sanders is a ceramic artist based in the Pacific Northwest. Her main interests are:

  • Family
  • Ceramic Arts and Sciences
  • Biohacking
  • Fine Dining
  • Modernist Cooking

Vickie goes through the entire process of making ceramics from sculpting the clay to mixing and applying glazes.

She first sculpts the clay to her liking, then fires it in the kiln, and finally uses her own hand-mixed glazes to create beautiful undertones.
